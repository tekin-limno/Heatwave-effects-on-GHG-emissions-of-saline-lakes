# Heatwave-effects-on-GHG-emissions-of-saline-lakes
Dataset accompanying the paper “Heatwave effects on GHG emissions of saline lakes.” https://doi.org/10.1007/s00343-026-5188-3

This repository contains the datasets supporting the study “Heatwave effects on GHG emissions of saline lakes.” The data correspond to measurements collected during mesocosm experiments investigating the effects of heatwaves and warming on greenhouse gas fluxes in saline lake systems. The datasets support the analyses and results presented in the associated paper.

Repository contents

meta_data.csv
Metadata linking mesocosm labels, units and mesocosm identifiers used in the experiments.
heatwave_exp_1.csv
Dataset from the heatwave experiment containing measurements of greenhouse gas fluxes and environmental variables.
heating_exp_2.csv
Dataset from the heating experiment containing measurements of greenhouse gas fluxes and environmental variables.
